The game is set in the cave and the main goal is to escape the cave with the treasure which is protected by a number of monsters in one of the rooms. It is dark in the cave and the flashlight is mostly needed to navigate through it. Flash light battery may die but also recharged by picking new batteries. Player must energy that diminishes as they move or do other things in the cave that draws energy. To keep the energy up, some intakes like food and water helps. Player can type in commands to play. One key tip is to type "help" to get reminded about them.
    Here are the available commands: 
        - go [north/south/east/west] ;
        - turn [on/off] flashlight ;
        - change flashlight battery; 
        - view inventory; 
        - pick up [item] ;
        - put down [item] ;
        - eat [food]; 
        - drink water ;
        - open water bottle ;
        - close water bottle; 
        - kill monsters;
Other than those, the code will tell the player to try again since it did not understand players command
The main challenges in this game are the monsters, energy levels and the flashlight battery.
